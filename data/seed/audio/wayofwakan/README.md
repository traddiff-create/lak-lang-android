## Source Description

Lakota language I & II textbooks and interactive audio tapes, originally prepared for college courses at Black Hills State College in the late 1970s, later used at Dakota Wesleyan University.

### Native Speakers (Audio Recordings)
- **Mary Young** — Sicangu Lakota
- **Billy Burnette** — Oglala Lakota
- **David Mathieu** — Course instructor

Recordings were made at Black Hills State College studios and simulate classroom language lab exercises.

### Orthography Note
Materials use a custom orthography developed for college classrooms, reflecting terminology common to Cheyenne River reservation communities. May differ from LLC Standard Orthography.
