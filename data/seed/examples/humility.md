english: Humility

---

Unsiiciyapi is one of the core Lakota virtues. It teaches that a person should not place themselves above others, and that true strength comes from serving the community rather than seeking personal glory.

Humility is taught through stories like those of Iktomi, where the consequences of pride and deception are revealed through humor and wisdom.
