Mitakuye Oyasin expresses the Lakota understanding that all living things are interconnected. It is spoken in ceremony and daily life as a reminder that every action affects the whole web of relations.

This phrase encompasses the Lakota worldview that humans are not separate from nature, but part of a larger family that includes animals, plants, the earth, and the spirit world.
