# Library of Congress — Public Domain Lakota Photographs

Historical photographs of Lakota people, places, and culture from the Library of Congress Prints & Photographs Division.

## Attribution

All images are in the **public domain** with no copyright restrictions.

**Source:** Library of Congress Prints & Photographs Division
**API:** https://www.loc.gov/pictures/search/?q=lakota+indians
**License:** Public Domain — free to use, share, and modify without permission

## Contents

- 56+ photographs (1870s-2020s)
- Subjects include: portraits, community life, historical sites, cultural events, Pine Ridge, Standing Rock
- Sensitivity filter applied: graphic Wounded Knee Massacre images excluded

## Usage in App

Suitable for:
- Vocabulary flashcard imagery (people, places, cultural items)
- Cultural module illustrations
- Story screen context images
- Cosmos node visuals (Person, Place, Ceremony directions)

## Review Status

All images enter the pipeline as `review_status: "draft"`. Community partner review required before display to learners to ensure culturally appropriate presentation.

## Content Integrity

Image titles and descriptions are from LOC metadata only — no AI-generated Lakota text or cultural descriptions.
