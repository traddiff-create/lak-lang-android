Iktomi is the trickster figure in Lakota tradition. Through his misadventures, the people learn important lessons about humility, honesty, and the consequences of foolish behavior.

In this story, Iktomi tries to use clever words to deceive others, but his tricks always come back around. The lesson teaches that words carry power and responsibility.

Iktomi stories are shared in winter, during the time of storytelling. They are meant to make people laugh while also passing on important values about how to live in good relation with others.
